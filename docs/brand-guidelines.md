# Quant Pulse Brand Guidelines

## Brand architecture

- Public brand: Quant Pulse
- Downstream validation partner: QuantLab Research
- Internal validation engine: QuantLab Core

## Positioning

Quant Pulse is the upstream signal layer for QuantLab Research.

Its role is to detect, filter, prioritize, summarize, alert, and archive market-relevant signals so they can be translated into disciplined downstream validation.

## Core relation

- Quant Pulse proposes the hypothesis
- QuantLab Research validates the intent
- QuantLab Core requires the evidence

## Brand principles

- upstream signal layer
- editorial discipline
- research intent generation
- risk awareness
- product prioritization

## Voice

Use:

- sobrio
- preciso
- analitico
- orientado a senal
- orientado a investigacion

Avoid:

- news aggregation as a product goal
- trading picks
- hype
- marketing language
- generic crypto media tone

## Visual direction

Quant Pulse should look like a signal radar that complements QuantLab Research:

- more editorial than executional
- more scanning than validation
- still disciplined and technical

### Visual cues

- dark analytical surfaces
- cyan / blue accents
- visible signal hierarchy
- concise cards and summaries
- strong emphasis on priority and classification

## Framing rule

Quant Pulse proposes the hypothesis. QuantLab Research requires the evidence.
