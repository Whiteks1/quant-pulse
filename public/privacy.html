Quant Pulse

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 4, 2026

Quant Pulse provides a public static news feed and editorial summaries. The Quant Pulse GPT may access this public feed to retrieve published items, executive brief points, and watchlist information.

What data is accessed

The GPT action only accesses publicly available Quant Pulse feed data hosted at: https://whiteks1.github.io/quant-pulse/data/pulse.json

Personal data

Quant Pulse does not intentionally collect, store, sell, or share personal user data through this public feed action.

How the action is used

  • Retrieve published news items
  • Retrieve executive brief summaries
  • Retrieve watchlist items
  • Support editorial analysis inside ChatGPT

Data retention

The static feed itself does not create user accounts and does not retain personal interaction data. Any interaction with the GPT is subject to OpenAI's own product policies.

Third-party services

Quant Pulse is hosted on GitHub Pages. Public content delivered through GitHub Pages may be subject to GitHub's infrastructure and policies.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated if Quant Pulse adds new integrations, authentication, analytics, or backend services.

Contact

Contact through the project repository: https://github.com/Whiteks1/quant-pulse/issues
